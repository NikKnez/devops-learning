=== Init System Timeline ===

1970s-2000s: SysV Init
- Traditional Unix approach
- Runlevels
- Sequential startup
- /etc/init.d/ scripts

2006-2014: Upstart (Ubuntu)
- Event-based
- Parallel startup
- /etc/init/*.conf
- Ubuntu-specific

2010-Present: systemd
- Most Linux distributions
- Unit files
- Dependency management
- /etc/systemd/system/
